Clubs, Extracurriculars, and Societies I was a part of in Highschool

Interact Club

Interact Club is a Rotary-sponsored service club for young people. I participate in community service projects and develop leadership skills through volunteering.

ASCAP

ASCAP stands for Avon School and Community Ambassador Program, one that can only be joined apon invatation as a result of strong academic work. This club has allowed me to be able to collaborate with my school to host events such as ribbon cuttings or community involved gatherings. We also as a club are able to work with other schools on projects that benefit the people of our district.

DECA Club

Freshman year I joined DECA. DECA has prepared me to be an emerging leader and allowed me to learn the ins and outs of entrepreneurship in marketing, finance, hospitality, and management. As a member, I competed in business events such as Marketing Management to develop real-world skills that are essential for the future.

Computer Science Honors Society

In CSHS, I was privelleged with the opprotunity to be an attributing member to this society. This group has shown me the ropes when it comes to advanced coding mechanisms to incorperate in programs and also being able to strongly steward the up and coming coders in lower grade levels. We were given opprotunity to visit Middle Schools and inspire to potentially choose Computer Science as their chosen field.

Speech & Debate

Speech and Debate has helped me develop public speaking, critical thinking, and argumentation skills through competitive events and team collaboration.

Chess Club

As a member of Chess club I experienced what its like to take on some highly skilled individuals leading me to experience struggle in something I am passionate about. I had a lot of fun in this group, made new friends and most importantly learned more about the art of chess
